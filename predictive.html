Predictive WiFi Design

We take floor plans for your location and use those, along with your other specifications and requirements, to design WiFi coverage for your building.

This can be done for existing buildings, or as part of the construction or pre-construction process for new locations.

We provide you with detailed plans for WiFi Access Point placements in order to provide a properly functioning wireless network for your location.
